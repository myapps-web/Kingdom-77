### 🎁 1. Giveaway System (2,200 lines)

Advanced giveaway system with entities (points) and templates support.

#### 🗄️ Database Layer (500 lines)
- ✅ **Giveaway Entity** - Complete giveaway data model
- ✅ **Entry Tracking** - MongoDB-based entry management
- ✅ **Winner Selection** - Fair random algorithm with weights
- ✅ **Entry Validation** - Requirements checking & limits
- ✅ **Templates System** - Reusable giveaway templates
- ✅ **Collections:** `giveaways`, `giveaway_settings`, `giveaway_templates`

#### ⚙️ Core System (700 lines)
- ✅ **Giveaway Creation** - Full customization support
- ✅ **Template-Based Creation** - Quick setup with saved templates
- ✅ **Entities Calculation** - Weighted entries (cumulative/highest modes)
- ✅ **Winner Selection** - Random with point-based weights
- ✅ **Requirements Validation** - Roles, level, credits, account age
- ✅ **Auto-Notifications** - DM + channel announcements
- ✅ **Reroll Functionality** - If winner doesn't respond
- ✅ **Embed Builders** - Custom footer, colors, images

#### 💬 Discord Commands (1,000 lines - 11 commands)
- ✅ `/giveaway create` - Create new giveaway (with template selector)
- ✅ `/giveaway list` - List active/ended giveaways
- ✅ `/giveaway end` - End giveaway early & pick winners
- ✅ `/giveaway reroll` - Reroll winners if needed
- ✅ `/giveaway delete` - Delete giveaway permanently
- ✅ `/giveaway edit` - Edit details (prize, duration, winners)
- ✅ `/giveaway entries` - View all entries with user info
- ✅ `/giveaway winners` - View selected winners
- ✅ `/giveaway stats` - Statistics (entries, participants, avg)
- ✅ `/giveaway template` - Manage templates (create/list/delete/favorite)
- ✅ `/giveaway use-template` - Use saved template

#### 🌟 Entities System (Points)
- ✅ Assign points to roles (1-100 points)
- ✅ 1 point = 1% extra win chance
- ✅ Two calculation modes:
  - **Cumulative:** Sum all role points
  - **Highest:** Use highest role only
- ✅ Weighted entries system
- ✅ Advanced statistics (avg points, max points)

#### 📋 Templates System
- ✅ Create reusable giveaway templates
- ✅ Save all settings (prize, winners, duration, entities, requirements)
- ✅ Full customization (colors, images, footer, emoji)
- ✅ Template selector dropdown in `/giveaway create`
- ✅ Favorite templates (⭐)
- ✅ Usage statistics per template
- ✅ **FREE with all tiers!** 🎉
